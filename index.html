About Me

Hey there! My name is Xander Goodwin and I’m a recent graduate of RIT's Game Design and Development program.


As a designer, I feel most inspired by games that immerse me. I want to create systems that hide in plain sight and meld with the world around them. When players are taught the rules of the world implicitly, it will naturally inspire them to think outside the box, applying real world logic to in-game obstacles. I seek out games like this because despite being fully aware of what a game is and how it’s made, I always want to feel like the world I’m adventuring through is alive.
